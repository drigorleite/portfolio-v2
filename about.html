About Me

I'm Rodrigo Leite, a Brazilian developer currently living in Michigan. My journey into tech wasn’t linear — and that’s what makes it real.

I grew up surrounded by law — my father and siblings are all lawyers. So, naturally, I followed that path too. But deep down, I was always drawn to tech. I admired people like Steve Jobs not just for the products, but for how they thought. I loved the idea of creating something that could impact others.

I eventually decided to give tech a real shot. At first, I studied IT Management in Brazil while keeping law as a backup. But soon, it was clear — coding was where I belonged. I loved the logic, the freedom, and the challenge. Today, I’m pursuing a degree in Applications Development in the U.S. and building real projects that solve real problems.
